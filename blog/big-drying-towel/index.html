The 'Big One': Mastering the 60x90cm Drying Towel

By MOSHAKHA Experts

Standard microfibre towels usually come in 40x40cm (16x16 inches). For buffing wax, this is perfect. For drying a bonnet the size of a ping pong table? Not so much.

Enter The Big One: The 60x90cm MOSHAKHA Drying Towel.

Why Go Big?

Unlike other steps in detailing, drying is about speed and capacity.

  1. Speed: You want to get the water off before it dries and spots. A 60x90cm towel covers more than 3x the surface area of a standard towel per swipe.
  2. Capacity: A larger towel holds more total water. A 40cm towel might get saturated after half a car, forcing you to wring it out (which is hard with thick towels). The 60x90cm drinks the entire ocean and asks for more.

Handling the Beast

Using a towel this large requires a different technique. do not scrunch it up!

  • The Throw & Drag: Unfurl the towel completely. Throw it like a parachute over the flat panel (hood, roof, or windshield). Grab two corners and pull. It dries everything in its path instantly.
  • The Fold: For side panels, fold it in half or thirds. This creates a thick, pillowy cushion that feels incredibly safe against the vertical finish.

Ideal For:

  • Large SUVs (Land Cruiser, Range Rover)
  • Pickups
  • Vans
  • Or simply anyone who wants to dry their sedan in less than 60 seconds.

Stop wringing. Start drying.
