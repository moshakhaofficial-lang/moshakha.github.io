The Art of Detailing: 500 GSM vs 800 GSM Microfibre Explained

By MOSHAKHA Experts

When it comes to automotive detailing, the quality of your tools defines the quality of your finish. At MOSHAKHA, we believe that understanding the science behind your equipment is the first step towards achieving a showroom shine. One of the most common questions we receive is: “What is the difference between 500 GSM and 800 GSM, and which one do I need?”

Today, we dive deep into the world of Gram per Square Metre (GSM) to help you make the right choice for your vehicle.

What is GSM?

GSM stands for Grams per Square Metre. It is the standard measurement for the density and weight of a fabric. In the context of microfibre cloths:

  • Higher GSM typically means a thicker, fluffier, and more absorbent towel.
  • Lower GSM usually indicates a lighter, often shorter-pile towel that provides more “bite.”

But remember: More isn’t always better—it’s about suitability for the task.

The 500 GSM: The Versatile Workhorse

Our 500 GSM Premium Microfibre Cloth is designed to constitute the perfect balance between absorbency and handling. It is substantial enough to feel luxurious but manageable enough to fold and control easily.

Best Uses for 500 GSM:

  1. Polish & Wax Removal: The medium pile length is excellent for biting into haze and removing cured wax or polish residue without clogging instantly.
  2. Interior Cleaning: Perfect for dusting dashboards or cleaning leather seats where you need agility.
  3. Spray Sealant Application: It absorbs just enough product to spread it evenly without soaking it all up.

Verdict: If you need one towel to do almost everything, the 500 GSM is your go-to.

The 800 GSM: The Luxury Buffer

The 800 GSM Ultra-Plush Microfibre is where utility meets luxury. These towels are significantly thicker and softer, typically featuring a specialized “shag” or split-weave pile.

Best Uses for 800 GSM:

  1. Final Buffing: After you’ve removed the bulk of a wax or coating, use the 800 GSM for the final wipe. Its long fibers trap any remaining dust specs away from the paint, ensuring a scratch-free finish.
  2. Quick Detailers: When using a quick detail spray on sensitive paint, the extra cushion of the 800 GSM acts as a safety barrier against swirls.
  3. Waterless Washing: The high capacity for holding dirt deep within its long fibers makes it safer for waterless wash techniques.

Verdict: The 800 GSM is the specialist. It’s the towel you bring out when the paint is perfect, and you want to keep it that way.
